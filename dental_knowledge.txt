Services - Scaling and Polishing
==================================
Scaling and polishing is a professional teeth cleaning procedure. It removes plaque, tartar, and stains that regular brushing cannot remove. The process uses ultrasonic tools to break down tartar buildup on teeth and along the gumline. Polishing is done after scaling to smooth the tooth surface. It is recommended every 6 months for good oral hygiene. It helps prevent gum disease, cavities, and bad breath. Cost: NRS 1,200 - 3,000.

Services - Root Canal Treatment
=================================
Root canal treatment (RCT) is performed by Dr. Aakash Karmacharya, the clinic's MDS Endodontist. It is done when the inner pulp of a tooth becomes infected or damaged. The procedure involves removing the infected pulp, cleaning and shaping the root canals, and sealing them. Root canal treatment saves a tooth that would otherwise need to be extracted. The procedure is done under local anesthesia so patients feel minimal pain. After treatment, a crown is usually placed over the tooth for protection. Cost: NRS 3,500 - 8,000 depending on tooth location.

Services - Braces and Clear Aligners
======================================
Orthodontic treatment is provided by Dr. Raju Shrestha, the clinic's MDS Orthodontist. The clinic offers traditional metal braces and clear aligners for correcting misaligned teeth and jaw problems. Braces are suitable for children, teenagers, and adults. Clear aligners are a nearly invisible alternative to traditional braces. Treatment duration depends on the severity of misalignment, usually ranging from 6 months to 2 years. Regular follow-up visits are required during orthodontic treatment. Cost: NRS 35,000 - 55,000 or above.

Services - Dental Implants
============================
Dental implants are provided by Dr. Nagendra Shris, the clinic's MDS Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon. A dental implant is a titanium screw placed into the jawbone to replace the root of a missing tooth. A crown is placed on top to look and function like a natural tooth. Implants are a permanent long-term solution for missing teeth. They preserve jawbone, improve appearance, and restore chewing ability. The process usually takes several months including healing time. Implants require healthy gums and sufficient jawbone density. Cost: NRS 50,000 or above per implant.

Services - Crown and Bridges
==============================
Dental crowns are caps placed over damaged or weakened teeth to restore their shape, size, and strength. Bridges replace one or more missing teeth by anchoring artificial teeth to adjacent natural teeth or implants. The clinic offers metal crowns (NRS 3,500), metal ceramic crowns (NRS 5,000 - 8,000), and zirconia crowns (NRS 10,000 - 15,000). Crowns are commonly placed after root canal treatment or for cracked teeth.

Services - Tooth Extraction
=============================
Tooth extraction is performed when a tooth is too damaged, decayed, or infected to be saved. It is also done for wisdom teeth, overcrowded teeth, and preparation for orthodontic treatment. The procedure is done under local anesthesia. Dr. Nagendra Shris handles complex surgical extractions. Pricing: Front tooth NRS 500 - 1,000, Back tooth NRS 800 - 1,500, Wisdom tooth NRS 2,000 or above, Children NRS 100 - 500.

Services - Tooth Fillings and Restoration
==========================================
The clinic offers GIC fillings (NRS 800 - 1,500), composite tooth-colored fillings (NRS 1,000 - 2,000), MRGI fillings (NRS 1,200), and temporary fillings (NRS 500). Services - Dentures
=====================

Common Dental Problems Treated
================================
Tooth decay and cavities: treated with fillings or root canals.
Gum disease (gingivitis and periodontitis): treated with scaling and deep cleaning (NRS 1,200 - 3,000).
Toothache: can be caused by cavities, infection, or cracked teeth.
Missing teeth: replaced with implants (NRS 50,000+), bridges, or dentures.
Misaligned teeth: corrected with braces or clear aligners (NRS 35,000 - 55,000+).
Broken or chipped teeth: restored with crowns or composite fillings.
Tooth sensitivity: treated based on underlying cause.
Bad breath: often caused by gum disease or poor hygiene, treated with cleaning.
Wisdom tooth pain: managed with extraction (NRS 2,000+).

A: A crown covers and protects a single damaged tooth. A bridge replaces one or more missing teeth by anchoring artificial teeth to neighboring teeth or implants.
